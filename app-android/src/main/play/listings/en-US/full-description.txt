This app adds Transit Windsor buses information to MonTransit.

The app contains the planned schedule as well as real time predictions from windsor.mytransitride.com.

Transit Windsor buses serve Windsor in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Transit Windsor and the City of Windsor.
http://www.citywindsor.ca/opendata/Pages/Open-Data-Catalogue.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-windsor-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Transit Windsor and the City of Windsor.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time schedule predictions from windsor.mytransitride.com
